I am a postgraduate student majoring in English Linguistics and Applied Linguistics at the School of Foreign Studies in Nanjing University.
My research interest lies especially in the issues of Second Language Acquisition from the perspectives of language processing and social culture.
I have also started to learn about the methods in research that is based on large-scale data, mainly focusing on the corpus approach and sentiment analysis.
